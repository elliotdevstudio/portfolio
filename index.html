'Square' feature - payroll reporting

This is a passion project currently in development, and was inspired by one of the difficulties I faced while running a restaurant. My objective is to create an additional feature for the 'Square' point-of-sale software using React and Recoil for state management, closely modeling the UX/UI that has contributed to its widespread popularity and with which I am highly familiar. It aims to solve a common problem for restaurant managers faced with the responsibility of distributing gratuities among different staff members who all contribute to the collective effort of providing great customer service. A CI/CD pipeline will be integrated at the time of deployment to enable a user to test the application in real-time. The image above is intended as a visual reference for the finalized iteration.

Technologies used:  React.js, HTML, Tailwind, JSON





FIG TREE - a hackathon project

This collaborative hackathon was hosted by General Assembly in May 2023, with a theme of 'Digital Equity and Accessiblity'. Our team of two developers and two UX/UI designers determined that the ubiquity and affordability of mobile smart devices opened some ground for exploring ideas, and with guidance of publicly available peer-reviewed research we concluded that our product should aim to assist individuals facing significant economic or housing challenges. To effectively manage the extensive range of desired components and functionalities, we focused on developing a minimum viable product (MVP) that included user authorization, a basic profile screen allowing users to create personal profiles, and a landing page featuring publicly accessible routes to various resources generated during our brainstorming sessions. For a few other additional endpoints that fell outside the scope of our project, we created mock-ups for presentation purposes, incorporating user interactions such as drop-down menus and basic animations. Our team was one of the smallest groups, after half of the initial participants needed to disengage. This was a great experience that challenged us on a professional level.

Technologies used:  Express.js, React.js, MongoDB, Netlify, CSS modules, HTML





FEEDBACK - A replica of Facebook's 'comment' feature

A full-stack, Node.js server using Express and a noSQL database that implements a few features such as 'auth', CRUD functionality, and REST-ful routing. This was a collaborative project done with one other GA classmate, with the goal of reverse engineering any part of a known or widely used application of our choosing. We went with the 'comments' functionality of 'Facebook', but given our constraints we decided to put together a simple app for sharing story prompts between interested writers who could then offer their feedback, hence the title. This is currently not a deployed project, but very soon everything posted in this section will have a properly working demo--and there will be more on the way!

Technologies used:  Node.js, Express, MongoDB, SASS, HTML5
