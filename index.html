FEEDBACK

A full-stack, Node.js server using Express and a noSQL database that implements a few features such as 'auth', CRUD functionality, and REST-ful routing. This was a collaborative project done with one other GA classmate, with the goal of reverse engineering any part of a known or widely used application of our choosing. We went with the 'comments' functionality of 'Facebook', but given our constraints we decided to put together a simple app for sharing story prompts between interested writers who could then offer their feedback, hence the title. This is currently not a deployed project, but very soon everything posted in this section will have a properly working demo--and there will be more on the way!

Technologies used:  Node.js, Express, MongoDB, SASS, HTML5
